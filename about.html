Our commitment

Statement of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

Metis Consulting Group is firmly committed to creating an accessible, welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment.

We recognize that oppressions based on race, ethnicity, creed (e.g., religion, belief system, or absence thereof), class, gender, sexual orientation, gender orientation, immigration status, country of origin, religion, mental health status, age, and ability are systemic in our society. These form a system of interlocking oppressions that are mutually reinforcing and cannot be addressed in isolation.

We acknowledge and appreciate the whole humanness of our employees, guests, clients, vendors, and partners, and value our interdependence. Our perspective is that best practices ensuring fairness and inclusiveness are a moving target, and therefore we embrace a methodology of constant re-evaluation of context, impact, and inclusiveness of policies, procedures, and business practices.
